Blake's Background

Upbringing:

I was born in Oak Lawn, Illinois on February 28th, 1997. Though initially living in Oak Lawn for some time, my family relocated to Huntley, Illinois. It is here where I initially started my grade school education.

After a few years of school, we relocated once more to Chicago, Illinois, where I would eventually graduate from grade school.

Teen Years:

Shortly after graduating from grade school, my family once again relocated to Chicago Ridge, Illinois where I would attend High School.

It was in high school that I began joining various clubs and participating in several activities. In addition to the drama and speech teams, I was a member of my high school's National Honors Society.

While in high school, I explored as many technology courses as I possibly could. Not only did technology always fascinate me, but now I was actively looking forward to coming to school to build on my knowledge in this area.

Because of my interest in technology and my performance in high school, I was admitted to the Illinois Institute of Technology on a scholarship. I still regard the day I learned this as the proudest I've ever been.

Current Aspirations:

Though initially I began my education at IIT as a Computer Science student, I switched to the Information Technology & Management major. This was due to the fact that what I had experienced in my CS classes was not what I was expecting. I was hoping for a more hands-on education, and this is what I saw was being offered by the ITM department.

My main interests are software development and managing database management systems.

Currently, I am working towards a specialization in data management and analytics in addition to the bachelor's degree in ITM.

Future:

Though the future is uncertain, I plan on pursuing a master's degree from IIT after my initial graduation.

My ideal future job have to be that of a database administrator.

In regards to location, I do not plan on leaving the Chicago area any time soon.
